Statement of Purpose

This app is designed to

  • Guide you in creating a Statement of Purpose
  • Allow you to define your values and beliefs
  • Inspire you to set goals that are in line with your statement

This is an assignment to create a verbal representation of “you” and who you hope to become. It is an idea to aspire to, it is a direction to go. It is not concrete and unmoveable, it is fluid, it is malleable.

Defining your values and beliefs will help you create this statement and then you may want to set goals that are in line with your statement.

Let's get started

To begin, you will answer several questions to get you thinking about who you are and what direction you want to go, then you can create your own statement in your own words or use a customizable template.

Creating a Statement of Purpose helps me to...

  • find focus in my life
  • dig deeper within myself to discover and define my values and beliefs
  • define my future and what I want in life
  • see who I am in relation to others
  • see who I am in relation to myself
  • refocus when I get off track
  • to see myself as I am and develop who I want to become
