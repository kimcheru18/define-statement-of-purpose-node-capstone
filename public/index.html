Creating a Statement of Purpose will help you...

Find your focus

Discover and define your values and beliefs

See who you are in relation to others

Determine how you can use your strengths to serve others

Your assignment...

Define your purpose. It doesn't have to be lengthy. It will be something you want to review often.


This is an idea to aspire to, it is fluid, and malleable. You can revisit it often and revise it as your path evolves.

You will be guided through the following...

1 Think through what is important to you

2 Define your values and beliefs

3 Create a Statement of Purpose

4 Set some goals that are in line with your statement

Step 1

Here are some questions to get you thinking about who you are and what direction you want to go, then you can create a Statement of Purpose in your own words or use a template.

Your answers will autofill into the provided template and then you may alter it if desired.

Instructions: Read the question and then complete the sentence.

What do I want to have accomplished in my lifetime for myself and for others?

It is my purpose to...

What strengths and talents do I posses?

I will grow and develop my...

Who are the people that I value most?

The people that are most important to me are...

What are some ways I can enrich my life and the lives of those that I love?

I will strive to...

Step 2.

Separate each value and belief with a comma to create a list.

What are my core values?

Values: a person's principles or standards of behavior; one's judgment of what is important in life.

What are my beliefs?

Belief: trust, faith, or confidence in someone or something.

Step 3.

Review Your Answers and create your Statement of Purpose

As you think about your answers, pick 3-4 main themes or ideas over all. Then begin to craft your Statment of Purpose. You can combine several ideas into one sentence if you would like.


You can create your own SOP free-style or use the template that is provided. If using the template, once complete, feel free to alter it to fit your needs.


Once you are finished, write or print it out your Statement of Purpose and place it where you can see it everyday. Your values and beliefs will be listed below. If desired, you can make a list of goals that are in line with your SOP. It will also be displayed below.
