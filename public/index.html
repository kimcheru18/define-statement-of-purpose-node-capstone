Why create a Statement of Purpose?

It helps to...

Find your focus

Discover and define your values and beliefs

See who you are in relation to others

Determine how you can use your strengths to serve others

Your assignment...

Create a verbal representation of your purpose in life. It doesn't have to be lengthy. It will be something you want to review often.


This is an idea to aspire to, it is fluid, and malleable. You can revisit it often and revise it as your path evolves.

You will be guided through the following...

Think through what is important to you

Defining your values and beliefs

Creating a Statement of Purpose

Setting goals that are in line with your statement

To begin...

You will answer a few questions to get you thinking about who you are and what direction you want to go, then you can create a Statement of Purpose in your own words or use a customizable template.

You may have several answers for each question.

What do I want to have accomplished in my lifetime for myself and for others?

What strengths and talents do I posses?

Who are the people that I value most?

What are some ways I can enrich the lives of those that I love?

Separate each value and belief with a comma to create a list.

What are my core values?

What are my beliefs?

Review Your Answers

As you think about your answers, pick 3-4 main themes or ideas over all. Then begin to craft your Statment of Purpose. You can combine several ideas into one sentence if you would like.


You can create your own SOP free-style or use the template that is provided. If using the template, once complete, feel free to alter it to fit your needs.


Once you are finished, write or print it out your Statement of Purpose and place it where you can see it everyday. Your values and beliefs will be listed below. If desired, you can make a list of goals that are in line with your SOP. It will also be displayed below.

Statement of Purpose

It is my purpose to help my friends and family by developing my talents and contributing to others in a kind manner.


I will make my world a better place by becoming the best version of myself, using my talents and encouraging others to follow their dreams


I want those closest to me to see me as a good and kind person but I realize that what is most important is that I accept myself as I am.


I want to bring joy into the lives of those that I love.
