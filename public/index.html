Your assignment...

Create a brief (3-4 sentence), verbal representation of “you” and who you hope to become.


This is an idea to aspire to, it is a direction to go. It is not concrete and unmoveable, it is fluid, and malleable.


This Statement is not about creating a business plan or a list of things to complete. It is introspective, a way to discover who you are at your core.


You will be guided through the following...

  • defining your values and beliefs
  • creating a Statement of Purpose
  • setting goals that are in line with your statement

Why should I create a Statement of Purpose? It helps me to...

  • find focus in my life.
  • dig deeper within myself to discover and define my values and beliefs.
  • define my future and determine who I want to be in life.
  • see who I am in relation to others.
  • see who I am in relation to myself.
  • refocus when I get off track.
  • to see myself as I am and develop who I want to become.

To begin...

You will answer several questions to get you thinking about who you are and what direction you want to go, then you can create a statement in your own words or use a customizable template.
