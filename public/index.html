Statement of Purpose

This app is designed to

  • Guide you in creating a Statement of Purpose.
  • Allow you to define your values and beliefs.
  • Inspire you to set goals that are in line with your statement.

This is an assignment to create a brief verbal representation of “you” and who you hope to become. It should only be 3-4 sentences long.

It is an idea to aspire to, it is a direction to go. It is not concrete and unmoveable, it is fluid, it is malleable.

This Statement is not about creating a business plan or a list of things to complete. It is introspective, a way to see who you are at your core.

Defining your values and beliefs will help you create this statement and then you can set goals that are in line with your statement.

Let's get started

To begin, you will answer several questions to get you thinking about who you are and what direction you want to go, then you can create a statement in your own words or use a customizable template.
